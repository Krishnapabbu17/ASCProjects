Spider-Man: Brand New Day Villains

About the Movie

Spider-Man: Brand New Day is an upcoming Spider-Man movie. Here are some villains that I would like to see Spider-Man fight.

Click here to learn more about Spider-Man

Villains I Want to See

  1. Green Goblin
  2. Venom
  3. Doctor Octopus
  4. Scorpion

Green Goblin

Green Goblin is one of Spider-Man's most dangerous enemies. He uses advanced technology, explosives, and a flying glider.

Venom

Venom is connected to an alien symbiote. He has super strength, sharp teeth, and many abilities similar to Spider-Man.

Doctor Octopus

Doctor Octopus is a scientist who controls four powerful mechanical arms. He is extremely intelligent and difficult for Spider-Man to defeat.

Scorpion

Scorpion wears a powerful suit with a mechanical tail. His strength and armor make him a serious threat to Spider-Man.
